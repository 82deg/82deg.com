About

Customer insight meets business strategy.

I’m a Senior Product Manager in fintech with 15+ years in UX and product leadership. I bridge customer insight with business strategy — driving digital innovation, leading global cross-functional teams, and delivering impactful solutions with measurable results.

My focus today is AI products and transformation. The last 18 months I’ve been leading the evolution of Backbase.io into an AI-first knowledge hub — retrieval-augmented generation, role-aware delivery, and the quiet engineering that turns AI demos into products teams actually rely on.

I founded 82 Degrees in 2009 as an independent studio; it’s now the home for selected freelance AI product engagements outside of Backbase, and for the side projects I ship when no one’s paying me to.

Stanford Online Product Management (XPROD110, 2024). Based in Amsterdam.

What I do

Three ways I help teams ship AI.

  • 01

    Strategy & discovery

    Translate customer insight and business strategy into a prioritised AI product roadmap. Sharp theses on where AI actually earns its keep, tested against real users and honest evals — not wishful thinking.

  • 02

    Teams & delivery

    Lead cross-functional, globally distributed product teams. Set the operating rhythm across design, engineering, data, and QA — so shipping AI stops being a heroic act and starts being a cadence.

  • 03

    Impact & measurement

    Take features from prompt to production with guardrails, evals, and the KPIs that matter. Boring, rigorous, compounding — exactly what AI products need to stop being demos and start delivering results.
